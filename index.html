What we know so far about Circuvirus? The virus is a circle and it gets bigger when it gets more dangerous and infectious. The only way to cure this is to implement medicine. If the virus gets too out of hand then it will take over the world and the cure will no longer work. Once the human is infected it is said that painful seizures occur and the person dies. Governments scramble to contain the outbreak, implementing strict quarantine measures and pouring resources into research efforts. Dr. Nexus leads an international coalition of scientists, racing against time to unravel the mysteries surrounding Circuvirus. Their findings unveil startling truths about its origins, suggesting potential links to environmental factors and human activity.)
